ESCAPE ROOM SOCIAL

You and your team have been captured and must escape. You find yourselves two floors underground with many locked doors between yourselves and freedom. Each door must be unlocked with a code.


In each room you will need to solve a puzzle, riddle or problem to reveal the code to the next door. All codes are made up of capital letters and/or numbers


You will be given the code to open Door 1 shortly, where the game will begin.


Rules of play

Teams will receive 10 points for being the first to reach different map milestones.

  • First team to finish floor 1
  • First team to finish floor 2
  • First team to finish floor 3

If your team gets stuck on a puzzle there are hints available.
Please request hints in your individual team chats for a hint on any particular door.

  • Hints will cost your team two points each
